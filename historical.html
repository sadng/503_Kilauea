Historical Eruptions

1790 to 1982

With many eruptions throughout 1790 to 1982, this map below showcases a few of the notable historical lava flows footprints. From eruptions contained within the Halemaʻumaʻu crater and the Kilauea Iki crater, to those that have changed the islands geomorphology completely. With the basemap provided, you can see how these historical flows are still visible upon todays surface.
Data collected from the U.S. Department of Interior, United States Geological Survey (USGS) ScienceBase Catalog, USGS Data Releases.
